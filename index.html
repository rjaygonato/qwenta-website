Does Qwenta POS work offline?

Yes. Qwenta POS supports both Online and Offline modes, allowing you to continue selling even if your internet connection is temporarily unavailable.

What businesses can use Qwenta POS?

Qwenta POS is ideal for restaurants, cafés, grocery stores, sari-sari stores, pharmacies, retail shops, resorts, and many other growing businesses.

Yes. Qwenta POS works with compatible barcode scanners for faster and more accurate checkout.

Can I use my own tablet or computer?

Absolutely. Qwenta POS is optimized for Apple and Android tablets and desktop computers, making it easy to set up using your existing hardware.

Yes. We provide installation assistance, system setup, training, and continuous updates to help your business run smoothly.
